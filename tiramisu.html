MY TRADITIONAL TIRAMISU

INGREDIENTS

  • 5 egg yolks
  • 220 g of powdered sugar
  • 500g of mascarpone
  • 500ml of cream
  • savoiardi
  • coffee
  • bitter cocoa
  • PREPARATION TIME: 20 minutes
  • COOKING TIME: in the fridge for a couple of hours

Put 5 egg yolks, 120g of powdered sugar, 500g of mascarpone cheese in a bowl and blend. In another bowl mount 500mI of cream with 100g of powdered sugar. Combine it all and mix. Take a baking dish and put a little cream and make a layer with the ladyfingers dipped in hot coffee. Make a layer of cream, one of savoiardi and in so doing up to end of the dough. On top of a layer of cream add the bitter cocoa. Put in refrigerator for a couple of hours: serve
