MY SOFT APPLE CAKE

INGREDIENTS FOR 4 PEOPLE

  • 1 kg of apples
  • 200 g of flour 00
  • 150 g of sugar
  • 80 g of butter
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 of milk
  • 1 sachet of baking powder
  • The grated zest of 1/2 lemon
  • 2 tablespoons of breadcrumbs
  • 1 tablespoon of powdered sugar
  • DIFFICULTY: Low
  • PREPARATION: 30 minutes
  • COOKING: 1 hour

Peel the apples and cut them into slices. Whisk with one for a long time whisk the eggs with the sugar until they are swollen and foamy. Add the flour, milk, and zest to the mixture of lemon and finally the yeast, then mix everything well until you get a mixture with a homogeneous consistency. Grease a pan with the necessary amount of butter, sprinkle it with the breadcrumbs and pour in the prepared mixture.
