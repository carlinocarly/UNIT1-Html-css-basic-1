MY CRUNCHY CHEESECAKE

INGREDIENTS FOR 6 PEOPLE

  • 100 g of butter
  • 250 g of dry biscuits (or rusks)
  • 20 g of sugar
  • 5 eggs
  • 1.2 kg of ricotta
  • the grated zest of 1 lemon
  • 400 g of organic sugar
  • 1 sachet of vanillin
  • 3 tablespoons of 00 flour
  • salt
  • DIFFICULTY: Medium
  • PREPARATION: 30 minutes plus 1 hour of cooling of the base and 1 hour and 30 minutes cooling down of the preparation
  • COOKING: 1 hour and 15 minutes

Prepare the base of the cake. Blend the biscuits with the sugar and the melted butter. Pour the mixture into a hinged pan, squeezing it well so as to cover the base and edges with a uniform layer with a thickness of 0.5 cm. Cook in the preheated oven at 180 ° C for 6-7 minutes; then let it cool. Prepare the filling. Beat the ricotta with an electric whisk until it becomes a soft cream. Mix the vanilla and lemon zest and add a little at a time, sugar, flour and a pinch of salt. Finally incorporate the eggs, one at a time, stirring after each addition. Spread the cream obtained on the biscuit base and pass the preparation in a preheated oven at 200 ° C for 7-8 minutes. Lower the temperature to 120 ° C and continue cooking for 1 hour. Let the cake cool in the oven, then transfer it to the refrigerator for about 1 hour. Decorate, to taste, with powdered sugar and lemon zest and serve.
