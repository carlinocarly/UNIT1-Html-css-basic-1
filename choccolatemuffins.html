MY GREEDY CHOCOLATE MUFFINS

INGREDIENTS FOR 4 PEOPLE

  • 160 g of corn flour for quick polent
  • 60 g of flour 00
  • 100 g of dark choccolate
  • 30 g of cocoa powder
  • 1 of whole milk
  • 1 egg
  • 1 sachet of yeast
  • 50 g of butter 100 g of sugar
  • salt

In a bowl, sift the corn flour with the wheat flour. Add the yeast sachet, sugar, cocoa, grated chocolate and a pinch of salt. Over low heat, gently melt the butter, then, when it is completely melted but before it starts to sizzle, slowly combine it with the other ingredients. Also add the egg shelled and poured milk. Mix carefully until when the mixture is smooth and homogeneous. Grease a mold prepared for 4 muffins, or as many single-portion molds suitable for cooking in the oven. Distributed then spoon the dough and cook the muffins in the oven already hot at 200 C for about 20 minutes. After this time remove the sweets from the oven, let them cool and turn them out.
